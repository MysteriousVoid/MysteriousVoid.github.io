Portfolio

  • Low code apps

    Product Management

    Problem Statement

    Sales rep were giving heavy discounts which was affecting products profitability.

    Intuition

    To understand the root cause behind this, we investigated past deals, looked at the average discounts, ran basic checks to find any country specific bias and even talked to sales people to understand the reality from the front line.

    After all these analyses it came out that the real missing piece was pricing governance.

    Solution

    • ● Cleaned the whole pricing list of around 800+ products. Here we performed cost based analysis and competitor's product pricing.
    • ● Built an inhouse Deal Pricing Calculator (Tableau) to standardise the quote generation process.
    • ● Enforced a deal review process that was actively monitored by deal review committee (which included people from pre sales, sales, product management, finance)

    Results

    A complete end to end cleanup of quote creation process backed with an in-house product which ensures that pricing mechanism for quote generation is applied uniformly by all sales reps across the globe

    Impact

    • ● Attained 50+ active users for the Deal Pricing Calculator.
    • ● Played a key role in generating business growth; & an increase of 1.2 million $ in the revenue.
